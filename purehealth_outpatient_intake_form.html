Privacy Practices

Notice of Privacy Practices — Summary

PureHealth uses your health information to provide care, process payments, and operate our facility. Your information may also be used for:

  • Communicating with family/representatives (unless you object)
  • Workers' compensation, law enforcement, and public health as required by law
  • Appointment reminders and health-related marketing
  • De-identified research with appropriate oversight

Your rights: You may request restrictions, access your records, request amendments, obtain a disclosure accounting, and file complaints with us or the Secretary of HHS — with no retaliation. A full copy of our Notice of Privacy Practices is available upon request from the facility Medical Records Director.
